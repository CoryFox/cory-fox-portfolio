SoloProtect combines lone-worker devices, live monitoring, and operational response tooling. The work sat in a technically demanding environment where **clarity, speed, and engineering reliability** all mattered.

My role covered both **UX direction and production delivery**. I worked on desktop monitoring tools, geolocation-led experiences, and the first iteration of the newer app experience, then pushed approved work through into a C# .NET and Xamarin codebase.

The result was a tighter link between design decisions and shipped product behaviour in a space where interface quality had direct operational consequences.
