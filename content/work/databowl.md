Databowl is a lead-validation and management platform with configuration-heavy journeys and a lot of room for user error. When I joined, the product was hard to move through and difficult to trust.

My role blended **UX architecture, Angular delivery, and product cleanup**. I worked on the journeys that mattered most to adoption: onboarding, campaign setup, validation logic, and export configuration.

The value of the work was not visual polish alone. It was making a messy internal-feeling system behave more like a credible SaaS product that users could configure with confidence.
