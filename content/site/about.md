I'm a full stack developer with more than six years of experience across SaaS, UX, and product delivery.

Most recently I was UX/UI Lead at FourJaw, a manufacturing analytics startup, where I worked across discovery, interface design, design systems, frontend modernisation, and hands-on product delivery in a Python, Flask, and MongoDB environment. That mix matters to me. I am comfortable working in Agile product teams, close enough to research to understand the problem properly, and close enough to implementation to help the solution ship well.

Earlier in my career I worked across SoloProtect and Databowl in roles that blended UX, UI, frontend, and supporting backend delivery. That included Angular and Postgres work at Databowl, plus C# .NET MVVM/MVC delivery at SoloProtect. Those roles taught me how to build for real product constraints, not idealised case-study conditions.

I now also teach Design and Technology, which has sharpened my thinking around clarity, sequencing, accessibility, and cognitive load. That perspective carries straight back into product work. I am comfortable with Git-heavy delivery, Linux-based workflows, and working across the stack, but I am still a designer at heart, so I care as much about visual quality and interaction feel as I do about whether the code holds up.
